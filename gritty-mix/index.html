Make a clean, flat horizontal cut across the top of the rootstock.

Drag the slider to position the scion's vascular ring over the rootstock's ring.

↔ Scion Position ⚠️ Not aligned

Press the scion down and wrap with grafting bands to secure the union.

Place the graft in a warm (22-28°C), bright spot with no direct sun. Wait 2-4 weeks for the union to heal.
